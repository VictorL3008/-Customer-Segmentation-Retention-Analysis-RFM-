In the highly competitive e-commerce sector, acquiring a new customer is **5 to 25 times more expensive** than retaining an existing one. This project analyzes transactional data from a UK-based online retailer (2009-2011) to identify customer segments and develop targeted retention strategies.

Using the **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)** technique, I segmented the customer base to answer critical business questions:
* Who are our most valuable customers?
* Which high-value customers are at risk of churning?
* How can we personalize marketing campaigns to maximize ROI?

3.  Optimize marketing budget by targeting the right audience.
